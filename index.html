Official Endorsement

Penang MOE Endorsed

"EGK deliberately introduced the AR module in Year 3 because it is 100% aligned with the 2017 KSSR Review DSKP. It avoids excessive screen exposure for young children and ensures technology is used only when it genuinely enhances understanding — the best example of 'technology for pedagogy', not 'pedagogy for technology'."

Developed in active consultation with the Penang Deputy Director of Education Department, EGK STEM AR is fully aligned with the Malaysian Ministry of Education's STEM Action Plan 2024–2026 and the Malaysia MADANI education vision.

About the Author

Built from a Dream

"We started with a single dream: to let every Malaysian student be able to 'hold' the planet, 'enter' the human heart, and 'bring to life' an electric circuit — all within a regular classroom. Today, after research and development, that dream has become a reality through 59 AR modules and the EGK STEM MasterApp."

Isaac Khor Eng Gian is the founder of EGK Microelectronic Solutions Group Sdn. Bhd., based in Penang, Malaysia. The EGK STEM AR series was developed in direct consultation with the Penang Deputy Director of Education and is acknowledged by the Ministry of Education Malaysia's Private Teaching & Learning Materials Evaluation Committee.

Beyond STEM, EGK has built a full ecosystem of educational technology including the AI Adventure series, Anti-Bully Careline, Humanitarian Resilience Initiative, and Malaysia's most comprehensive transport apps suite.
